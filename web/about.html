Inspired from a Christmas gift from my son, which was a container filled with tiny scraps of paper. Each scrap of paper had a They Might Be Giants song written on it. Every day of 2024 I got to pull a new piece of paper out of the container. Now that 2024 is over, I want to keep the song of the day going.
