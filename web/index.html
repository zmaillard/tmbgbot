They Might Be Giants of the Day Bot

A bot to deliver a They Might Be Giants Song to your feed. Every day!

Inspired from a present my son gave me for Christmas of 2024, which consistent of a container filled with pieces of paper with a They Might Be Giants song written on it. Every day of 2024, I got to pull a new song out of the "hat". Now that 2024 is over, and my container full of songs is empty, I am putting together a bot to do the same thing for the people of BlueSky.
